Our solutions

We introduce concepts that enable you to do exponentially more using a fraction of your energy, time and cost.

Hybrid Cloud

In today's business architectures, a common theme is finding ways to leverage the internet scale offerings from cloud providers while complying with regulations of storing client data locally.

We want the best of both worlds, and the challenge with this model is managing this hybrid cloud as a single entity. Streamlining the application delivery and operational processes.

In these scenarios we build frameworks that ties together the different pieces in your service delivery stack. Using orchestration, one deployment cycle can be scaled up to one hundred, on-demand.

Service Compliance

Compliance is a key part of any organization handling sensitive data or delivering a highly critical service to the public. Maintaining security and operational compliance is usually a rigorous process requiring multiple departments to follow strict procedures.

We think compliance should be a continuous and transparent part of the daily operations, giving you instant feedback on compliance status. As soon as a change is rolled out, automated processes verify that the infrastructure remains in a compliant state. The same model applies to security and operational stability.

Operational Efficiency

The daily operations is a mix of technical tasks and business processes. In common, most of them require a high degree of manual and repetitive labor. It can be information retrieval, new deployments, upgrading of existing systems or simple idle waiting for a linear task to complete.

Using telemetry we are able to highlight the outliers in a given process chain. Where are the pain points and how much can be gained by thinking differently. Maybe 30% of it can be fully automated? Let's spend energy on that.
