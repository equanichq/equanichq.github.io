Our mission


EQs mission is to enable you to stay ahead of the curve. To give you an edge.

Using enterprise-grade open software, automation and telemetry we meticulously enhance your current state of operations enabling you to spend more time innovating towards the future.

We believe the services of tomorrow will be delivered using white-labeled open architectures, intelligently data-driven processes, simplistic application designs, interconnected physical objects and hybrid clouds - all beautifully automated and scalable.

Our services

You should focus on what you're great at. We'll make sure you excel at doing just that.

  • Consulting

    For over a decade we have delivered network and systems consulting services to enterprises, financial services, retail, service providers, governmental and public institutions.

    We are certified on the major platforms and thrive in multi-vendor environments. We look at IT infrastructure from all angles, ensuring the technical opportunities are aligned with the business goals.

    We understand the challenges that the current legacy solutions impose on your ability to innovate and compete, but even there we reveal improvement opportunities.

  • Telemetry

    Everything consist of metrics, be it in business processes, services or applications. We are surrounded by them in everything we do.

    Each and every one of these metrics are an indicator of a current state, a pulse.

    By applying analytics systematically over time, we discover patterns that enable you to answer the important questions:

    Does your service provider deliver according to SLA? How are you aligned for future growth? Which part of the daily operations can be optimized?

  • Automation

    From business processes to infrastructure and applications, all of these components can be automated using tools available today.

    Automation allow us to augment human capabilities by increasing productivity, ensuring service reliability and delivering a higher level of quality with fewer errors.

    By automating repetitive tasks we enable ourselves to spend more time on what we humans are great at - innovation and creativity, analytical processes, communication, decision making, strategy and planning.
